SEO

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) in digital marketing is the practice of optimizing websites to rank higher in search engine results. It helps increase organic traffic and visibility for businesses online.

Content

Content in digital marketing involves creating valuable and relevant material like blogs, videos, and posts to attract and engage a target audience. It helps build trust, improve SEO, and drive conversions.

Display

Display in digital marketing refers to visual ads shown on websites, apps, or social media platforms. These ads include banners, videos, and images to attract user attention and promote brands.

Social Media

Social media in digital marketing involves using platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter to promote products, engage with audiences, and build brand awareness. It enables direct communication and targeted advertising.
